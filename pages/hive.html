Mobile First — HIVE UX/UI/Graphics 2023

Context & Problem

Conceived by CGI stakeholders, HIVE is a mobile-first application designed to connect employees through shared talents and professional skills. The platform enables consultants to exchange expertise or request upskilling opportunities. I led the end-to-end user journey design, translating complex functional requirements into intuitive high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes.

Tools

Figma
Illustrator

As a Designer I did...

  • Analysis: Audited analyst-led specification documents to define core UX functionalities.
  • Prototyping: Architected user flows and high-fidelity mockups for the app's primary features.
  • Visual Design: Developed the HIVE branding, including logo design and visual assets.
  • Stakeholder Management: Hosted recurring co-creation workshops to bridge the gap between business needs and user experience.
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Presented design solutions to the engineering team, facilitating a smooth transition from concept to code.
