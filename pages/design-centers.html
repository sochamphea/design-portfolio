Design Centers Communication 2025

Context & Problem

The Design Centers, a global entity within Thales Group (located in Singapore, Brazil, Paris, and more), focus on enhancing the user experience of the Group's products. To support their growth, a need for stronger internal promotion emerged. My role was to increase their visibility and strengthen their communication through dedicated internal tools and platforms for employees.
