Journey Map UX Research 2025

Context & Problem

As part of the optimization of smart card manufacturing processes (SIM cards, payment cards) within Thales Group's production sites, a continuous improvement initiative was launched.

In collaboration with a Project Manager and a Product Owner specialized in process optimization, workshops were conducted to identify key friction points. A journey map was then developed to align the different production sites and prioritize improvement opportunities.

Tools

Miro board

As a Designer I did...

  • Structured and organized information for each stage of the process based on existing research.
  • Reviewed completed steps with the Product Owner and defined next priorities and action items.
  • Validated the journey map with stakeholders to prepare upcoming co-design workshops.
  • Planned and coordinated workshops with cross-functional teams involved in the manufacturing process.
