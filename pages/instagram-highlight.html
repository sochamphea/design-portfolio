Instagram Highlight Graphics 2024

Context & Problem

For my personal Instagram, I aimed to create strong visual consistency across my travel story highlights while strengthening my graphic design skills by creating the vector illustrations myself.

Tools

Illustrator

As a Designer I did...

  • Inspiration Research: Conducted visual research to explore current highlight styles and emerging design trends, identifying relevant references to inform the creative direction.
  • Symbol Research: For each country and city I visited, I researched and selected meaningful symbols and landmarks that best represent their cultural and visual identity.
  • Vector Design: Redrew key illustration outlines, simplified shapes, and vectorized selected images. Finalized the pictograms by unifying stroke weights and visual details to ensure strong graphic consistency across the entire set.
  • Export & Integration: Prepared final assets and integrated them into the application.
