Government Website UX/UI Design 2024

Context & Problem

The Curateur public du Québec is dedicated to protecting incapacitated individuals while upholding their rights, autonomy, and wishes. As a UX and CX Designer within Agile and CX4 teams, I focused on improving the user experience for the Annual Management Account (AMA). My work centered on streamlining the application process to ensure it is simple, clear, and deeply human-centric for those in vulnerable situations.

As a Designer I did...

  • Formalized the service blueprint for the case-opening process, delivering a strategic report to streamline analyst workflows.
  • Designed end-to-end interfaces for the Annual Management Account (AMA) digital platform.
  • Led user testing preparation, including the creation of research protocols to improve user comprehension.
  • Iterated on UI designs through constant feedback loops with Product Owners and cross-functional analysts.
  • Ensured product-wide cohesion by collaborating with the design team on shared UI patterns and standards.
