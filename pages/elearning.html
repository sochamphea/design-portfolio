E-learning Tool UX/UI Design 2019

Context & Problem

Starting from an initial UI concept, I led the structural development of a comprehensive e-learning platform. My mission was to clarify and architect the end-to-end user journey, ensuring the flow was educationally sound. I designed the final HMI (Human-Machine Interface) to provide a cohesive, intuitive experience that prioritizes learning outcomes and user engagement.

Tools

Sketch

As a Designer I did...

  • Information Architecture: Developed a comprehensive sitemap and content hierarchy to organize complex educational data.
  • User Journey Mapping: Defined and presented the end-to-end user experience to stakeholders, ensuring a logical pedagogical flow.
  • Iterative High-Fidelity Design: Once the architecture was validated, I designed high-fidelity mockups through multiple feedback loops and refinements.
