Logo & Website idea Graphics 2016

Context & Problem

I spearheaded the creation of a boutique real estate agency that bridges the gap between property sales and interior design consultancy. My work involved defining a dual-purpose brand identity that appeals to both home buyers and sellers. I designed a cohesive digital ecosystem that showcases premium listings while highlighting the agency's unique value proposition: helping clients visualize and optimize their future living spaces through professional interior planning.

Tools

Illustrator
InDesign

As a Designer I did...

  • Market Research: Analyzed real estate trends and company values.
  • Identity Design: Created and iterated on multiple logo directions for the final brand selection.
  • UX Audit: Evaluated initial website concepts for usability and structural integrity.
  • Homepage Design: Designed the primary web interface to establish the brand's digital presence.
