Service Blueprint UX Research 2024

Context & Problem

24h Tremblant is a premier charitable event held annually in Mont-Tremblant, where teams relay for 24 hours—skiing, running, or walking—to raise funds for children's causes. As part of an internal strategic initiative, I led a UX/CX reflection aimed at optimizing the event ecosystem.

Tools

Miro board

As a Designer I did...

  • Service Blueprint: Designed a comprehensive service blueprint mapping the end-to-end timeline of the 24-hour event, visualizing the intersection between participant activities and behind-the-scenes operations.
  • Stakeholder Research: Conducted in-depth interviews with event managers to gather operational insights, followed by iterative validation sessions to ensure the accuracy of the documented workflows.
  • Optimized the newsletter ecosystem, streamlining the email journey to increase participant engagement and fundraising conversion.
  • Journey Mapping & Ideation: Performed comprehensive process mapping to identify friction points and proposed targeted UX solutions to enhance participant satisfaction and operational efficiency.
