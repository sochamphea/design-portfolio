Mobile Services UX/UI Design 2018

Context & Problem

Hamak, a peer-to-peer and professional services platform, sought to modernize a website that had remained unchanged since its launch. The project focused on optimizing navigation, clarifying the value proposition, and introducing a refreshed visual direction.

The goal was to establish a renewed visual identity, enhance user journeys, and integrate a new service offering that involved professionals directly in the mission creation process.

Tools

Sketch

As a Designer I did...

  • Conducted a thorough analysis of the existing user journey to identify friction points and improvement opportunities.
  • Designed a detailed information architecture for both the website and mobile application to ensure consistency and scalability.
  • Organized guerrilla testing sessions to validate hypotheses and prioritize key improvements.
  • Collaborated within an Agile framework alongside developers and the Product Owner to align design decisions with technical and business constraints.
  • Delivered redesigned mockups informed by insights from user testing and an in-depth review of the existing platform.
