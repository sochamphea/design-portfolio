Resto & Bar Menu Graphics 2014

Context & Problem

As part of the launch of a family-style restaurant, a clear and appealing communication strategy was developed to showcase the menu and effectively engage with customers.

In a separate project for a local neighborhood bar, the objective was to promote its event programming and cultural agenda to the local community, creating visibility and driving attendance.

Tools

Illustrator
InDesign

As a Designer I did...

  • For both projects, my role was to design communication materials that would build credibility and foster trust with customers.
  • Created a brand logo and integrated it into a cohesive, well-designed menu.
  • Designed a flyer presenting the full event program, ensuring clarity, visual impact, and local appeal.
