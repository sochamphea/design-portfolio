Rewards Tool UX Design 2018

Context & Problem

Groupe UP is a global leader in employee benefits and reward solutions. To strengthen engagement on the Pulse Standard platform, I led a strategic UX initiative focused on motivation and retention drivers. I designed tailored reward mechanics and loyalty loops specifically for sales teams, aiming to recognize high performance and foster long-term commitment through personalized incentive journeys.

Tools

Sketch

As a Designer I did...

  • Discovery & Planning: Collaborated with the Product Owner to translate « Reward and Loyalty » requirements into functional specifications. Defined the core UX framework for the Pulse Standard incentive ecosystem.
  • UX Architecture: Designed high-fidelity wireframes focused on Responsive Web Design, prioritizing accessibility and performance. Managed a rigorous iteration process to validate user flows and interaction models.
  • Design Team Integration: Established a collaborative workflow with the UI Designer to ensure the brand's visual identity enhanced the underlying UX strategy. Acted as the bridge between functional requirements and the final visual layer.
