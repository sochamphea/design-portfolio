Marine Tool — Melchior UX/UI/Graphics 2019

Context & Problem

Building on the Melchior initiative—a specialized system for ship-to-ship data transmission—I joined a senior UX Designer to finalize the platform's interface. Working from initial concepts, I focused on refining the HMI (Human-Machine Interface) to enhance visual clarity and modernize the interface. Additionally, I developed the visual identity and logos for the Alpha and Omega software suites, ensuring a cohesive brand ecosystem within the project.

Tools

Sketch
Illustrator

As a Designer I did...

  • Technical Discovery: Conducted briefing sessions with developers and technical leads to align design solutions.
  • UI Refinement & Evolution: Iterated on existing UI concepts to enhance visual clarity and modernize the interface.
  • Functional Simplification: Optimized complex HMI layouts to ensure critical data and software features are represented simply and intuitively.
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Partnered closely with the engineering team through rapid iteration cycles to ensure the final interfaces were technically feasible and user-centric.
