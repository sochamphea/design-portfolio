Insurance Flow UX Design 2016

Context & Problem

I led the UX optimization of the Mon Allianz Mobile (MAM) subscription journey, a service dedicated to the purchase and pairing of smart home security devices. Building on initial client wireframes, I designed a streamlined, cross-platform experience (iOS, Android, and Desktop) aimed at simplifying the technical setup while fostering clarity, trust, and a seamless end-to-end user journey.

Tools

Axure

As a Designer I did...

  • Collaborated closely with the client through communication and feedback platforms such as Basecamp and InVision to ensure alignment throughout the project.
  • Conducted a competitive benchmark analysis to identify market standards, best practices, and differentiation opportunities.
  • Designed new responsive screens, delivering high-fidelity wireframes for mobile and tablet experiences.
