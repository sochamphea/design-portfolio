Machining Tool — Liggo UX/UI Design 2021

Context & Problem

Liggo is a specialized startup focused on optimizing manufacturing production through advanced digital tools. Working alongside a Product Owner and a cross-functional engineering team, I led the continuous improvement of the platform's UX and visual identity. My role was to bridge the gap between complex industrial data and user efficiency, ensuring the tool remained high-performing, intuitive, and visually cohesive.

Tools

Figma

As a Designer I did...

  • Led UX Research & Strategy: Conducted interviews and ergonomic audits to define the product roadmap.
  • Information Architecture: Reimagined the platform's navigation and sitemap to handle complex manufacturing data more intuitively.
  • UI/UX Design: Designed and prototyped the new interface, from the login experience to the core navigation system.
  • Usability Testing: Facilitated UI testing sessions to validate the new interface and ensure a frictionless user experience.
  • Cross-Functional Evangelism: Presented UX/UI best practices to the team to foster a user-centric product culture.
