SEO Pages UX Design 2018

Context & Problem

The SEO team at Pages Jaunes aimed to develop editorial content for a broad audience to help users discover and connect with top-rated local professionals. The project involved designing scalable templates for editorial articles, the homepage, and city-level pages (e.g., Paris, Bordeaux). The focus was on creating a clear content structure, improving readability, and enhancing overall SEO performance. Through this work, I contributed to building a cohesive content framework that balanced user experience, editorial credibility, and search visibility.

Tools

Sketch

As a Designer I did...

  • Conducted a comprehensive needs analysis to align business objectives, SEO requirements, and user expectations.
  • Reviewed and optimized the site architecture proposed by the SEO team to ensure logical content hierarchy, improved navigation, and stronger search performance.
  • Designed high-fidelity mockups aligned with the Pages Jaunes brand guidelines, collaborating closely with an in-house UI Designer to ensure visual consistency and design system integration.
