Financial Tool UX/UI Design 2021

Context & Problem

The firm sought to modernize its legacy financial suite to improve usability and align with contemporary design standards. As a UX/UI Designer, I partnered with development teams to evolve the product interfaces, focusing on enhancing ergonomic efficiency and visual cohesion. My work transformed complex financial workflows into a more intuitive, user-friendly experience that meets the expectations of modern professionals.

Tools

Adobe XD

As a Designer I did...

  • Stakeholder Alignment: Conducted requirement gathering sessions with Product Owners to define project scope.
  • UI Exploration: Developed multiple UI concepts and visual directions to explore optimal layouts for complex financial data.
  • Design System Integration: Created high-fidelity mockups strictly adhering to Desjardins' brand guidelines and design system.
  • Interactive Prototyping: Built functional prototypes to demonstrate user flows and validate interactions with stakeholders and development teams.
