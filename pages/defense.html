Defense UX/UI Design 2025

Context & Problem

Within a sensitive institutional environment involving artificial intelligence technologies, I supported agile teams in improving mission interfaces designed for operators.

The objective was to optimize existing interfaces, design new configuration screens, and facilitate AI training processes in order to enhance operational efficiency.

Tools

Miro board
Figma

As a Designer I did...

  • Interface Design: Created and iterated wireframes and high-fidelity mockups in Figma
  • Co-creation Workshops: Facilitated collaborative sessions with scientists and subject-matter experts
  • Feature Definition: Defined key functionalities for upcoming interface releases
  • Stakeholder Interviews: Conducted interviews to gather insights and align expectations
  • Operator Journey Mapping: Mapped user journeys to better understand operator needs and workflows
