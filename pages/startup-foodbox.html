Start-up Food Box Graphics 2015

Context & Problem

Place du Village was a start-up focused on selling themed apéritif boxes and 100% Made-in-France products. The challenge was to design and produce the full range of communication assets, from materials for physical events to content tailored for social media platforms, ensuring a cohesive and distinctive brand presence across all touchpoints.

Tools

Suite Adobe
Photography

As a Designer I did...

  • Developed a strong understanding of the start-up's brand values and product range to ensure strategic and consistent design decisions.
  • Designed and set up product displays for physical events, enhancing visual merchandising and brand storytelling.
  • Created graphic assets for public-facing communication campaigns across multiple channels.
  • Proposed a new brand logo, along with distinct visual identities for each themed box.
  • Conceptualized a second version of the e-commerce website to improve structure, storytelling, and conversion.
  • Redesigned the box packaging to deliver a more cohesive, premium, and memorable unboxing experience.
